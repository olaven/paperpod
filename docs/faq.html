What can I save with Paperpod?

You will be able to save any article on the web as well as textual PDF files. More formats are planned.

How many articles can I save?

You can save as many articles as you want.

How can I save articles to Paperpod?

Paperpod will have a "one-click" browser extension and an equaly simple mobile app.

How can I listen to my saved articles?

You will get a personal podcast URL that you can add to the podcast player you already use. Any article you save will appear as a new episode.

Support for a dedicated player may release in the future.

Will Paperpod cost money?

Yes, Paperpod will have a affordable subscription. Having a transparent business model means no shady stuff like selling your data. To anyone. Ever.

A subscription model also allows for an open development, where anyone can check out Paperpod's source code if they want to :-)

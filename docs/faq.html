What can I save with Paperpod

You can save any article on the web :-)

Support for PDF's and custom content is also planned

How many articles will I save?

You may save as many articles as you want.

How will I save articles to Paperpod?

Paperpod will have a "one-click" browser extension and an equaly simple mobile app.< /p>

How can I listen to a podcast?

You'll get a personal podcast URL that you can add to the podcast player you already use. Any article you save will appear as a new episode.

Support for a dedicated player may release in the future.

Will Paperpod cost money?

Yes, Paperpod will have a affordable subscription. Being transparent about how we'll cover expenses means we're not having to do shady stuff like selling you data. To anyone. Ever.

A subscription model also allows for an open development], where anyone can check out Paperpod's source code if they want to :-)
